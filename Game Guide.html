Jumping Square - Full Guide

General Info

the game is infinite. there is no ending for the game.

there are 5 different musics that will played randomly when you start a new game. you can change to other selected randomly music by clicking the button "change music" or pressing the key "c" in game

How To Play

How To Survive?
in the game you need to stay away from crushing with the pipes and don't fall on the ground by Jumping. otherwise you will lose.
What Safe?
clouds,coins,flowers,bushes are safe to touch without losing!
How To Obtain Scores?
you get one score everytime you pass a pipe or collect a coin.

keys:

  • up arrow key/spacebar/mouse click - jump
  • enter key - full screen
  • c key - change music
